# BRANDING.md

## Product Name
ShortCircuit

## Tagline
Break long links. Instantly.

---

## Brand Personality

- Aggressive
- Fast
- Disruptive
- Slightly chaotic (controlled chaos)
- Developer-first

Feels like a tool that doesn’t ask permission.

---

## Brand Concept

A long URL is inefficient and bloated.

ShortCircuit doesn't "optimize" it —  
it **forces a faster path**.

Like electricity skipping the long route,  
your link jumps straight to the destination.

---

## Visual Direction

### Theme
Cyberpunk electricity (NOT soft neon aesthetic)

### Core Idea
Energy overload → forced efficiency

---

## Colors

Primary:
- #0B0B10 (near-black)
- #14141D (dark surface)

Electric Accents:
- #00FFF0 (electric cyan)
- #FF3B3B (electric red)

Optional highlight:
- #FFD93D (warning yellow — use sparingly)

Support:
- #8B90A5 (muted gray)
- #1F2230 (cards/borders)

---

## Typography

Headings:
- Space Grotesk / Sora (bold, tight)

Body:
- Inter

Accent:
- JetBrains Mono (for URLs, codes)

---

## Logo Direction

Symbol ideas:
- Lightning bolt cutting through a chain/link
- Broken circuit line (sharp angles)
- A "ZAP" diagonal slash through text

Keep it simple, iconic, sharp.

---

## UI Style

Layout:
- Centered, focused
- Strong contrast

Components:
- Slightly sharp corners (6–10px, less soft than usual)
- Thin glowing borders (cyan/red mix)

---

## Effects

- Electric flicker on hover
- Micro “zap” animation on click
- Fast transitions (100–200ms)

Use effects sparingly — impact > noise

---

## Interaction Feel

- Instant
- Reactive
- Snappy

Example flow:
Paste → instant validation  
Click shorten → quick "zap" compression animation  
Result appears immediately

---

## Copywriting Style

Tone:
- Direct
- Slightly aggressive
- Confident

Examples:
- "Kill the length."
- "Too long. Fix it."
- "Shorten it. Move on."
- "No delays. No fluff."

Avoid:
- corporate tone
- friendly/soft language

---

## Motion Direction

- Electrical pulse effects
- Sharp flick transitions
- Subtle glitch (only during actions)

Avoid:
- constant glitch spam
- slow easing

---

## Product Features (Initial)

- URL shortening
- Custom aliases
- Click tracking
- QR generation

Future:
- API for devs
- smart redirects
- link rules / automation

---

## Overall Feel

ShortCircuit should feel like:

"A tool that bypasses the system instead of playing by it."

Fast. Aggressive. Efficient.
